xMatters is an on-call and service reliability platform. Harness AI SRE syncs xMatters on-call schedules to identify responders for an incident.

## How AI SRE supports xMatters

- **On-call schedule sync:** AI SRE reads xMatters schedules and rotations to identify the current on-call responder for an incident.

## Set up xMatters

- Go to the [xMatters on-call integration](/docs/ai-sre/oncall/integrations/xmatters) to sync xMatters schedules with AI SRE.
