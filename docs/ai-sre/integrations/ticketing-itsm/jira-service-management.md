Jira Service Management (JSM) is Atlassian's IT service management tool for handling alerts, on-call, and incident response.

## How AI SRE supports Jira Service Management

AI SRE supports Jira Service Management through runbook actions. A runbook action creates JSM alerts during a response so responders are notified through their existing JSM workflows.

- **Runbook actions:** Runbooks create JSM alerts during a response. This capability may be gated for your account; contact Harness support if it is not available.

## Set up Jira Service Management

- Go to [runbook integrations](/docs/ai-sre/runbooks/integrations/overview) to configure JSM alert creation from runbooks. Detailed setup for the Jira Service Management runbook action is being published separately.
