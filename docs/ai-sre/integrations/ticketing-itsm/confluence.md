Confluence is Atlassian's team workspace for creating, organizing, and sharing documentation such as incident reports and postmortems.

## How AI SRE supports Confluence

AI SRE supports Confluence through runbook actions. A runbook action posts content to Confluence pages, creating a new page or updating an existing one using the HTML storage format.

- **Runbook actions:** Runbooks create or update Confluence pages during a response, so incident records and postmortems stay current without manual effort.
