Microsoft Teams is a collaboration platform for chat, meetings, and channel-based teamwork within Microsoft 365.

## How AI SRE supports Microsoft Teams

AI SRE supports Microsoft Teams through two mechanisms. Teams collaborate on incidents in Microsoft Teams, and runbook actions automate messaging and meetings.

- **Incident collaboration:** Teams coordinate incident response in Microsoft Teams, and AI SRE captures the conversation in the incident record.
- **Runbook actions:** Runbooks post messages and create meetings during a response.
