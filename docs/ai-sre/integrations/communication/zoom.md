Zoom is a video conferencing platform for hosting meetings, webinars, and incident bridge calls.

## How AI SRE supports Zoom

AI SRE supports Zoom through runbook actions. Runbooks manage the lifecycle of a Zoom meeting during a response.

- **Runbook actions:** Runbooks create meetings, get participants, and end meetings so responders can spin up and manage a conference bridge automatically.

## Set up Zoom

- Go to [Zoom runbook actions](/docs/ai-sre/runbooks/integrations/collaboration/zoom) to configure meeting creation, participant retrieval, and meeting termination from runbooks.
