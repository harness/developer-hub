Slack is a team messaging platform for real-time collaboration through channels, direct messages, and app integrations.

## How AI SRE supports Slack

AI SRE supports Slack through three mechanisms. Teams collaborate on incidents in Slack, run Slack commands to act on incidents, and automate messaging through runbook actions.

- **Incident collaboration:** Teams coordinate incident response in Slack, and AI SRE captures the conversation in the incident record.
- **Slack commands:** Responders run `/harness` commands to act on incidents directly from Slack.
- **Runbook actions:** Runbooks post messages and create or archive channels during a response.

## Set up Slack

- Go to [Slack runbook actions](/docs/ai-sre/runbooks/integrations/collaboration/slack) to automate messages and channel management from runbooks.
- Go to [Slack commands](/docs/ai-sre/get-started/slack-commands) to run `/harness` commands from Slack.
